The Slug Game

You are a slug, living in a beautiful lush garden somewhere on earth.

You and your slug companions like nothing more than having a good party, but unfortunately some necessary items have been picked up by a human and taken into "the house".

It is down to you to be the hero of the hour and get the party started once more.

Your task is to enter the house and find a disco ball and a party light.

To navigate the house, and interact with the things you encounter, you can use various commands. These are usually highlighted but there are some you may need to work out for yourself.

You will need to be quick on your mission: you have moisture level that will decrease over time. If you run out of moisture you will fall asleep and no doubt be found by a human.

Please enter your name, and press start
